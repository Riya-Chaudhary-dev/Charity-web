Our Vision

Our Mission

Education

Learning is the preparation of tomorrow, to uncover truths and defeat myths, to empower not one but an entire community. We believe everyone deserves the caress and comfort of knowledge to discover oneself but unfortunately, it comes with a price that has posed a difficulty for many capable candidates. Through several scholarships and school programs, we have provided aid to young brilliant minds who yearn for a better future.

Love & Care

As one season transforms into another, no mortal is immune to the bout of ailments and difficulties these changes bring along. Many homeless citizens suffer discomfort and disease that ultimately becomes the cause of their demise. We organize blanket distributions every winter to help our brothers and sister shield themselves in the warmth of love and care.

Guidance

Sometimes lending an ear or a shoulder is enough to bear the most trying of times and we believe in the power of speech that can sow the seeds of courage and diligence in the most tired and lost hearts. Talk sessions to inculcate moral sensitivity and spiritual guidance are held and encouraged throughout the community.

The Art of Altruism

Sheo Sharma

Founder, Philanthropist, Writer, Yoga Enthusiast
It was in the very early years of my life that I understood the unconquerable power of kindness and love, growing up in the era of British Raj, followed by the dawn of freedom while the economic stature of most families dwindled. Communities thrived on hope, while the youth struggled to find their ground. Education suffered, healthcare was non-existent and the future seemed bleak. Time passed and situations changed, yet one thought remained stuck. This organisation was that persistent thought, that undying wish to serve those who had faced the darker side of life, to share the love I have garnered over the past eighty odd years. To live and, to let live.
